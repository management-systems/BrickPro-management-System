🧱 BrickPro — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Last Updated: May 23, 2026

BrickPro ("we", "our", or "us") operates the BrickPro mobile application and web platform (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, mobile number, email address, factory name, and location when you sign up.
  • Business Data: Production records, dispatch/sales data, customer details, raw material purchases, labour information, expenditure records, and invoice data that you enter into the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for providing support and improving the Service.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and time spent for improving user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To authenticate your identity (OTP via email)
  • To send notifications about your account and subscription
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve and optimize the Service
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in India (ap-south-1 region). We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. JWT tokens are used for authentication.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal or business data with third parties. Your data is only accessible to:

  • You and your authorized team members (based on roles you assign)
  • Our support team (only when you request assistance)

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Workspace (SMTP): For sending OTP and notification emails
  • WhatsApp: For sharing challans/receipts (initiated by you only)
  • Expo Notifications: For delivering push notifications

6. Data Retention

Your business data is retained as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, your data is retained for 90 days before permanent deletion. You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data (PDF/Excel reports available in-app)
  • Opt-out of promotional notifications

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
