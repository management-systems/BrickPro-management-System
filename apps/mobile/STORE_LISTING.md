## Full Description

BrickPro is a complete brick factory management solution designed for brick kiln owners, managers, and supervisors in India.

**PRODUCTION TRACKING**
• Record daily brick production (raw & fired bricks)
• Track by brick type (Red Brick, Fly Ash, AAC Block, etc.)
• Shift-wise tracking (Morning, Evening, Night, Full Day)
• Monthly production summaries with charts

**SALES & DISPATCH**
• Create sale challans with ticket numbers
• Track customer payments (Paid / Partial / Credit)
• Automatic outstanding calculation
• Truck and driver details

**CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT**
• Maintain complete customer database
• View outstanding balances at a glance
• Complete transaction history per customer
• Quick "Sell Bricks" from customer profile

**LABOUR MANAGEMENT**
• Daily attendance marking (Present/Absent/Half)
• Per-brick rate & daily wage tracking
• Payment recording with mode (Cash/UPI/Bank)
• Monthly payment summaries

**EXPENSE TRACKING**
• 12+ expense categories (Electricity, Diesel, JCB, Transport, etc.)
• Payment mode tracking
• Monthly expense reports with category breakdown
• Fuel consumption monitoring

**REPORTS & ANALYTICS**
• Monthly profit & loss statements
• Expense breakdown by category
• Top customers by revenue
• Sales vs expense comparison
• Outstanding payment tracking

**ADDITIONAL FEATURES**
• Multi-factory support
• Hindi & English language
• Dark mode & Light mode
• Role-based access (Owner, Manager, Supervisor, Operator)
• Secure login with email/mobile + password
• Works offline with data sync

Perfect for brick kiln owners, managers, and supervisors who want to digitize their factory operations.
